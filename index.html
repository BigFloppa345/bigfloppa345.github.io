My favourite GTA Online Heists:

(The first one is my favorite, the third one is also my favorite, but not as much as the first one)

  1. Cayo Perico
    2. Here is the trailer for Cayo Perico Heist

    The Cayo Perico Heist is one of the most lucrative and challenging heists in GTA Online, added in December 2020.
    You head to the private island of drug lord El Rubio to steal valuable loot (from stacks of cash to jewelry and paintings).

  2. Doomsday Heist
    3. Here is the trailer for Doomsday Heist

    The Doomsday Heist is one of the most challenging and epic heists in GTA Online, added in 2017.
    Unlike other heists, this one is not just a robbery, but a fight to save the world from a techno-apocalypse.

  3. The Diamond Casino Heist
    4. Here is the trailer The Diamond

    The Diamond Casino Heist is one of the coolest and most varied heists in GTA Online.
    It was released in December 2019 and allows you to rob the Diamond Casino & Resort, where you can steal cash, gold, jewelry, or art.
